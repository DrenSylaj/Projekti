Cathedral

The Cathedral of Saint Mother Teresa in Pristina is a Roman Catholic cathedral constructed in Pristina, Kosovo. In 2007, the Government of Kosovo approved plans for the building. The cathedral is dedicated to the Albanian-Indian Roman Catholic nun and missionary, Saint Teresa of Calcutta.
